Codemate Ltd is a software development house specialized in creating web services and mobile applications. We aim to provide state-of-the-art software solutions to our customers by using our solid experience in modern technologies. We are located in Oulu, Helsinki, Dhaka and Bangkok. 

Now, we are strengthening our sales team. Would you be our new Sales Manager located in Helsinki?

Your ideal background is in a small or mid-sized software company and you have a proven track record in sales. Your focus will be in new sales following our sales strategy. We respect professional attitude and commitment to the visions of our clients. You have no fear of contacting and meeting high-level decision makers. You are convincing when discussing with business or technical oriented people. Keeping promises and being proud of your profession is what you stand for! And if you were good in numbers that would sound even better.

Finnish and English language skills are needed.

You have colleagues with technical and commercial background. This business is team work but we can rely on your self-driven attitude. Depending on your potential we are willing to give more responsibilities in the future. So, we really do want to invest in our personnel. Business is sometimes serious but we don´t forget that there should be a balance between job and freetime.  

Interested? Apply for this opportunity and send your CV with a salary request to careers-fi@codemate.com by 17.1.2014. Should you have questions please contact Janne Korvanen, Sales Director, 040-7048996, preferably January 16th at 15-16.

MOBILE DEVELOPERS

Codemate is growing. We are currently hiring mobile developers to reinforce our team in Oulu.

You have skills in at least ONE of the following technologies:

  • Android
  • iOS
  • Windows Phone 8
  • Web front-end

The applications we develop for our clients are either native mobile apps or web-tech hybrid apps. Typically the apps have a back-end service as well, so having skills in using different API's is a must. The wider knowledge you have on the before mentioned technologies, the better.

Fluent Finnish and English skills are required.

We offer truly interesting projects and tasks where you will learn new things all the time. We are a young, professional and fun bunch.

Now, please do send your CV and brief application to careers-fi@codemate.com
 

WEB FRONT-END DEVELOPER

We are looking for a web front-end developer to join our team in Oulu.

Your tasks include technical design and implementation of modern user interfaces for high-class web services. JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3 and responsive design tools and libraries are your weapons of choice. Back-end software experience (PHP/Python) is a plus. You will take part in client projects, which keeps you learning new things all the time.

If you are experienced in front-end web technologies and want to become a respected part of our team, don't hesitate to contact us. We offer competitive benefits, a good team to work with and possibilities to achieve new heights in your career.

Please send your CV and application to careers-fi@codemate.com

Cheers!

SOFTWARE ARCHITECT

We are looking for a capable Software Architect to join our team in Dhaka.

At Codemate you are involved with varying client projects, finding the best technical solutions and designing beautiful software systems that tackle the client's needs. You are an invaluable part of Codemate, giving the development team a clear plan to implement.

Your main responsibilities include software architecture design, effort estimations, process management and help in solving technical issues of the development teams.

The main techs we use are PHP/Drupal/Zend and JavaEE. And of course the wide set of open source techs that are needed for front-end, server side, databases, etc.

Codemate is a growing international company with great career opportunitities and self-development possibilities.

If you got what it takes, we welcome you to send your CV and application without delay to careers-bd@codemate.com

FRONT-END SOFTWARE DEVELOPER

We are looking for capable Front-end software specialist to join our team in Dhaka.

You will create beautiful and modern user interfaces for high-class web services. JavaScript, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3 and responsive design tools are your weapons of choice.

Codemate is an international software development company with HQ in Finland and main development site in Dhaka. We create state-of-art web and mobile solutions for our business customers, according to their needs. As Codemate is involved in project delivery, not with its own products, we have very wide range of the technologies to master. We also don't get bored of having to work with same stuff all the time. The main techs we have used for web systems are JavaEE and PHP/Drupal/Zend.

If you are experienced in front-end web technologies and want to become a respected part of our team, don't hesitate to contact us. We offer competitive benefits, a good team to work with and possibilities to achieve new heights in your career.

Send your CV and application without delay careers-bd@codemate.com
